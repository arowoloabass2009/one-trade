Privacy Policy

How ONE-TRADE collects, uses, and protects your data.

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

ONE-TRADE collects information you voluntarily provide when you subscribe to our newsletter, submit a contact form, or comment on our blog. This may include your name and email address. We do not require account registration.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and contact form submissions
  • To send newsletter updates if you have subscribed
  • To publish comments you submit on our blog (after moderation)
  • To improve our services and website experience

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (a PostgreSQL-backed platform) with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your browser session and preferences. No third-party advertising cookies are used. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may be affected.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@one-trade.com to exercise these rights.

6. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact our Data Protection team at privacy@one-trade.com.
