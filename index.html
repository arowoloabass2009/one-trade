❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ONE-TRADE investment services.

How does ONE-TRADE's managed investment work? ▾
Our expert fund managers handle all trade execution and portfolio management on your behalf. You deposit funds, choose your plan, and receive regular ROI payouts while our team actively manages your portfolio using proven strategies.
What is the minimum investment amount? ▾
Our Starter Plan begins at just $500 USD, making professional investment accessible to everyone. The Growth Plan starts at $2,500 and our Elite Plan at $10,000 for clients seeking maximum returns.
How often are returns paid out? ▾
Payouts vary by plan — monthly for Starter, bi-weekly for Growth, and weekly for Elite clients. All payouts are processed reliably and transparently to your designated account.
Is my capital safe with ONE-TRADE? ▾
Client capital is held in segregated accounts, separate from operational funds. We maintain strict risk management protocols, maximum drawdown limits, and comprehensive compliance procedures to protect your investment.
Can I withdraw my investment at any time? ▾
Yes. Withdrawals are processed within 2–5 business days. Notice periods may apply depending on your plan, and our team will guide you through the process. We believe in full transparency and client flexibility.
