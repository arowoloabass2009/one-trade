1. General Risk Warning

Forex, stocks, cryptocurrencies, and commodities are highly volatile instruments. Market conditions can change rapidly and unpredictably. There is no guarantee that any investment strategy will achieve its objectives.

2. Leverage Risk

Leveraged trading amplifies both potential profits and losses. A small adverse market movement can result in losses greater than your initial investment.

3. Market Risk

Financial markets are affected by numerous factors including economic data, geopolitical events, central bank decisions, and investor sentiment — many of which are unpredictable.

4. Liquidity Risk

Some markets may experience periods of low liquidity, which can result in wider spreads, slippage, and difficulty executing trades at desired prices.

Past performance of any investment, strategy, or trading system is not indicative of future results. Returns displayed on this website represent historical data and do not guarantee future performance.
